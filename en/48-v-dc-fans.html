MOBAIR 48 V DC
Duct and wall mounted fans

Duct models are available in ⌀200 and 250 mm sizes (48 V DC duct fan) and wall mounted fans for inlet (EFU-200 air supply unit) and oulet (EEU-200 exhaust unit). Fans are connected and operated by back-up batteries on site.
