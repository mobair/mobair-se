FAQ

Here you will find frequently asked questions about our products. If you can't find the answer you were looking for, please contact us.

What is an air supply heater and how does it differ from an AC?

An air-source heat pump circles indoor air without improving its oxygen levels but only its temperature. A supply air valve on the other hand improves not only temperature but also oxygen levels by bringing in cleaned, healthy and fresh outside air

What exactly is intake air supply?

It is the incoming fresh air which replaces the old air that exits typically from the kitchen, bathroom etc. It is very important that the incoming fresh air is pre-heated and filtered.

Into which room is an air supply fan supposed to be installed?

You must install a suitable air supply fan in a room where the ventilation needs to be improved. You can install an air supply fan into every room, or just to the ones that have ventilation problems. For example in homes, bedrooms are the most important rooms to have good air quality to facilitate good sleep and well-being.

How much electricity does it use?

Mobair 2030S air supply fan is very energy efficient and approximately uses only 1.0 W. Mobair 2010 and 2015 air supply heaters that have a dedicated heating element, use approximately 200 W on average. However, electricity used by the air supply heaters is compensated by smaller heating costs in general as the pre-heated supply air lessens the workload of other heating sources, e.g. radiators.

Do air supply fans interfere with the buildings existing ventilation system?

No. Air supply fans do not interfere with any existing ventilation system. However, it is required in apartment buildings as well as other buildings that air supply fans must not cause positive air pressure indoors. All Mobair products have specifically been designed to prevent this from happening, and the authorities have approved all Mobair systems.

Acoustic noise levels

All Mobair fans are very silent. This makes them ideal solutions for bedrooms; since sleep will not be disturbed by noises and fresh air supply will further facilitate good sleep. Acoustical noise for the models Mobair 2015 and 2030S is less than 27 dB(A). Our air supply fans are so silent that e.g. radiators can make more noise than the fan itself.

In addition, a silencer (AV2000) can be installed inside of the air duct to further reduce incoming outside noises in urban areas.

Can you install Mobair products in the same room with an AC?

Yes. It is very usual to use both of these devices at the same time. An air supply fan can only improve air quality.
